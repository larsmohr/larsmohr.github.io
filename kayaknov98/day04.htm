Log Entry
Relaxed on Jetty Island. I was worried about my shoulder pain, so I took a day off. It wasn't enough for a full recovery - much how my blisters would not heal in one day. I hope to be comfortable tomorrow. Today would have been excellent - the wind would have been at my back for the trip to Camano. Hopefully tomorrow too.

I spent today relaxing. I ate well, listened to my Charles Lindbergh book on tape, explored the island, dried things, repaired some damage to the hull, glued in the heel pad on the floor, played with the map and compass. It really started pouring in the afternoon and things got damp again.

I wish I could have called Sylvia today, and kept up the daily afternoon calls, but it's about 1hr each way to the phone. I'd like to rest my shoulders. Possibly I can get to the phone in the morning. I'll call Sylvia and then head for Camano.
