Tuesday, November 17, 1998.

Welcome! These pages document the construction of  a 17 foot kayak and her maiden voyage. After 127+ hours of work over 5 months, she was ready.

When my vacation started in late October, I spent the next 7 days completing the kayak, gearing up for her maiden voyage, training, and reading. Although we've done lots of hiking and canoeing, this is the first kayak camping trip for me.

Because I was heading out alone, and in winter, I was making careful safety preparations. In addition to flares and the other standard safety items, I was planning to paddle in my scuba diving drysuit, and I would be carrying a marine VHF radio.

The day after the kayak was ready, November 1st, I launched into the cool November air on a 200 mile journey to Vancouver Island with 20 days of food. I wore a big smile...
