Notes
This kayak is made from 8x4 plywood sheets and then stitched and glued together. While there are lots of positives to building this kayak, but I don't think I'll build another boat. It takes a lot of time - time I could have spent paddling if I just bought a used boat. I feel that money possibly saved has been overshadowed by lost wages also. What I mean is, if I had bought a boat and used the kayak-building time to study and learn to advance my career, I could be way ahead.

On the pro-build side, working in the shop was enjoyable. It is satisfying to have built my own boat (one of those life achievements). I now have the experience of building something from plans, working with fiberglass and resins, and have learned new tips and tricks and skills. Overall, it was rewarding and I'm happy to have built the kayak.
